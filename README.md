**Project Title**
Food Ordering Behaviour Analysis Using Tableau

**problem statement**
- The food delivery industry generates a large amount of customer and order data every day.
- Businesses need to analyze this data to understand customer behavior, delivery performance, restaurant performance, and ordering trends. 
- This project aims to transform raw food ordering data into meaningful insights through interactive Tableau dashboards to support better business decision-making.

**Objectives**
- Analyze food ordering trends.
- Identify top-performing restaurants and cuisines.
- Understand customer ordering behavior.
- Evaluate order quantity and sales performance.
- Visualize business data using interactive Tableau dashboards.
- Generate insights to support business decision-making.

**Dataset Description**

The dataset contains information related to food orders placed through an online food delivery platform.

- Dataset includes:
- Order ID
- Restaurant Name
- Food Category
- Cuisine
- Customer ID
- Order Date
- Quantity
- Price
- Total Amount
- Delivery Status
- Payment Method

**Key Insights**
- The highest number of orders were placed during peak meal hours.
- Certain restaurants consistently generated higher revenue than others.
- Popular cuisines contributed significantly to total sales.
- Digital payment methods were used more frequently than cash payments.
- Some months recorded noticeably higher order volumes.
- A small number of restaurants accounted for a large share of total revenue.
- Customer purchasing behavior showed recurring ordering patterns.
- High-value orders contributed significantly to overall business revenue.
- Delivery performance affected customer satisfaction and repeat orders.
- Interactive dashboards help management quickly identify business opportunities
